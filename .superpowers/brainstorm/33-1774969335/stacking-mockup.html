Speech Bubble Stacking Behavior

Interactive mockup — click "Say something" to see the stacking in action

NPC

Behavior Summary

  • New bubble appears at base (closest to character) with fade + slide-up animation
  • Previous bubbles smoothly slide upward to make room
  • Auto-completes typing on previous bubble when a new one arrives
  • Separator line (centered, 60% width) appears between bubbles
  • Each bubble expires independently after its own duration
  • When a bubble expires, it fades out and others slide down to close the gap
