Cross-Character Speech Zone (Habbo Style)

Two NPCs within 15 units — their bubbles push each other up to avoid overlap

How it works

  • Each character has their own bubble stack (newest at bottom, closest to character)
  • When two characters are within 15 world units, their stacks share vertical space
  • A new bubble from Character B pushes Character A's entire stack up with an empty gap (no separator)
  • Same-character stacking uses white separator lines
  • Pushed bubbles stay where they were pushed — they don't slide back when the pushing bubble expires
