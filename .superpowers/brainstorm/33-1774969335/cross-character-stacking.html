Cross-Character Speech Zone (Habbo Style)

Two NPCs within 15 units of each other — their bubbles avoid overlap by pushing up

How it works

  • Each character has their own bubble stack (newest at bottom, closest to character)
  • When two characters are within 15 world units, their stacks share vertical space
  • A new bubble from Character B pushes Character A's entire stack up by the height of B's new bubble (with empty gap, no separator)
  • When a bubble expires, it fades out — but pushed bubbles stay where they were pushed (no slide-back)
  • Same-character stacking still uses white separator lines; cross-character pushing uses empty space
