Rule 4: Characters follow their target during splits

If F was in Engagement 1 targeting B, and B splits off into Engagement 2 → F follows B into Engagement 2. Your engagement is wherever your target is.

Summary: Engagement Rules

1. Form
Two characters targeting each other create an engagement
2. Join
Targeting someone in an engagement → you join it
3. Split
When a subgroup's targets are all within each other, they form their own engagement
4. Follow
When engagements split, you go where your target goes
