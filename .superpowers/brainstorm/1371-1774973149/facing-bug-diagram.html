FIX: Defender's facing is locked to HIS chosen target (ATK 1). Only the defender's own AI can change who he faces. Attackers targeting him do NOT override his look direction.

Root Cause: Three Systems Fight Over Facing

1. CombatAILogic.UpdateFlip()
Every frame, flips character toward its current AI target direction
2. CharacterVisual.LateUpdate()
If _lookTarget is set, faces that target every frame in LateUpdate
3. SetPlannedTarget()
Called by each attacker, overwrites _lookTarget via SetActionIntent → SetLookTarget
